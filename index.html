TWO STEPS FROM HELL

Two Steps From Hell was founded by Thomas Bergersen and Nick Phoenix way back in 2006 when they started to join forces to write original music for movie trailers(trailer music). 13 years later, TSFH(Two Steps From Hell) had composed nearly millions of songs and movie ads. During these 13 years, they had first published their first album, Invincible, and made a genre called epic music. And now there are 14 public albums avaliable, catching millions of fans.
